About

Where strategy meets
storytelling

I am Praise Oluebube Okwuchi — and for over ten years I have been helping organizations find their voice, strengthen their reputation, and communicate with purpose. I hold a Master's degree in Public Management from Babcock University and a Bachelor's degree in French Language. My journey spans corporate communications, NGO leadership, policy advocacy, and development practice.

"I am not taking a piece of history back home — I've become a piece of it."

From growing social media communities from 7,000 to 22,000 in one year to mobilizing ₦7 million in private sector support through strategic campaigns, I bring the same commitment: understand deeply, communicate authentically, and execute with precision. I believe effective communication bridges gaps, builds trust, and creates lasting impact.
